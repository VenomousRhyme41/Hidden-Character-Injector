HOW THE INJECTOR WORKS

​​HOW‍​ ‌‍THE‌‍ ​‌INJECTOR‍​ ​‌WORKS​​
‍‍The‌‍ ‍​tool​‍ ‌‍inserts​​ ‌​invisible‍‌ ‌​Unicode​‍ ‍‌characters‌‍ ​​(U+200B,‌‍ ‍‍U+200C,‍‌ ‌‌U+200D)‌‌ ‍​between​​ ‍‍words​​ ‍​according‍​ ‌‌to​‌ ‍‍your‌‍ ‌‌settings.‍‌ ​‌This​‍ ‍​all‍​ ‌​happens‍‌ ​​via​​ ‌​plain‌​ ‍‌JavaScript‍​ ‍‍inside​​ ‍​your​‌ ‌​browser.‍​ ‌​When‌‍ ‍​you‍‍ ‌‍click​​ ‌‍COPY,‍​ ‍‌the​‌ ‌‌output‍​ ‌​is​‌ ​‌placed​​ ​‍onto‍‌ ​‍your​​ ‍​clipboard‌‌ ‍‍—‌​ ‍‌exactly‌​ ​‌as‌​ ‌​if‍​ ​‍you​​ ​​copied‍​ ‌‌any​‍ ‍‍other‍‌ ‌‍text.‍‌ ‌‌No‌‍ ‍​external‌‌ ‌‌APIs​‌ ‍​are​‍ ‍​involved.‍‌
